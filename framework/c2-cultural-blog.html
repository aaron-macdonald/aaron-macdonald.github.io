Emotional Intelligence

What learnings did I get from reading chapter one of the book "Search Inside Yourself"

I found out what Emotional Intelligence (EQ) is ... which is a good start

I found a list, and I like lists

  • Self-awareness: Knowledge of one's internal states, preferences, resources, and intuitions
  • Self-regulation: Management of one's internal states, impulses, and resources
  • MotivationL Emotional tendancies that guide or facilitate reaching goals
  • Empathy: Awareness of others' feelings, needs, and concerns
  • Social Skills: Adeptness to indicing desirable responses in others
